Tribute to Ratan Tata

Ratan's quote:

"I Don't believe in taking right decisions. I take decisions and make them right."

Ratan Tata served as the chairman of the Tata Group from 1991 to 2012. Under his leadership, the group underwent significant expansion and globalization, acquiring several international companies. Notable acquisitions during his tenure include Tetley, Jaguar Land Rover, and Corus Group. Ratan Tata is known for his business acumen, ethical leadership, and commitment to corporate social responsibility.

Apart from his contributions to the business world, Ratan Tata is involved in various philanthropic activities. He established the Tata Trusts, which are among the oldest and largest philanthropic organizations in India. These trusts work on various social and development issues, including healthcare, education, and rural development.

Ratan Tata is widely respected for his achievements in business and his commitment to making a positive impact on society. After stepping down as chairman, he continues to be involved in various business and philanthropic endeavors.
